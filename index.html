Step 6 - GPS Location (survey point - record before moving on)
Primary: Google Maps -> tap blue dot -> record the complete coordinates shown.  Fallback: NCIL -> MAP tab -> record LAT/LON exactly as displayed.  Do not round the values. Latitude negative e.g. -9.4318123. Longitude positive e.g. 160.0523456.
Step 7 - Tower GPS Waypoint
When to complete Step 7: Only if you can walk to within 10 metres of the tower base safely and without trespassing. If the tower is behind a fence, on private land, or on a slope you cannot safely reach, select Accessible: No and explain the reason.
GPS method: stand at the base of the tower before recording coordinates. While standing at or near the tower, open NCIL RAW and record the tower/cell values below directly from the phone. Do not rely on values remembered from an earlier participant survey.
NCIL tower/cell identity at tower site
Enter only the essential NCIL RAW values needed to link survey points to the serving tower/cell and explain findings: operator/PLMN, spectrum band, serving cell/sector, coverage strength and QoS signal quality.
NCIL signal values at tower site
Record the signal values shown by NCIL at the tower location. These help distinguish tower identity from signal quality.

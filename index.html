Step 6 — GPS Location (survey point — record before moving on)
Primary: Google Maps → tap blue dot → read coordinates.  Fallback: NCIL → MAP tab → read LAT/LON.  Always 4 decimal places. Latitude negative e.g. −9.4318. Longitude positive e.g. 160.0523.
Step 7 — Tower GPS Waypoint
When to complete Step 7: Only if you can walk to within 10 metres of the tower base safely and without trespassing. If the tower is behind a fence, on private land, or on a slope you cannot safely reach, select Accessible: No and explain the reason.
GPS method: stand at the base of the tower before recording coordinates. While standing at the tower, open NCIL RAW and confirm the eNB number matches Step 2A or 2B. Record a different eNB below, and note the operator name and tower type from the tower sign or plate.
Cell Tower Lookup — OpenCelliD

Enter MCC, MNC, and eNB from NCIL RAW tab to look up tower GPS coordinates from OpenCelliD. Requires a free API key from opencellid.org.
